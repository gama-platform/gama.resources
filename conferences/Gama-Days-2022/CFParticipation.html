Building on last year’s success, the GAMA Days 2022 is the 2nd conference, where users and developers of the GAMA modeling and simulation platform will have an opportunity to meet, present their work, expose their difficulties, propose enhancements and, more generally, exchange and collaborate on exciting topics related to GAMA!

GAMA Days 2022 deals with :

  • - new features for GAMA, or
  • - models that uses GAMA.
Additionally, presenters can choose, whether they want to:
  • - give a (live) demo of a GAMA feature or model that is presented in the slot right after the talk.
